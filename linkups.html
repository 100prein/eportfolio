Linkups

Connecting People. Empowering Communities

Introduction

Linkups is a company dedicated to helping people connect with outdoor activities, groups, and events in their local community. With the ongoing challenges of COVID-19, it's more important than ever to prioritize physical and mental health, and getting outside is a key part of that. Linkups is focused on making it easy and convenient for people to find and participate in safe, fun, and engaging outdoor activities that promote well-being and community connection.

Project Goals

  1. Introducing our platform to local businesses, groups, and event planners to list their outdoor events and activities through our platform.
  2. Launch a referral program to incentivize current users to refer their friends and family to the platform.
  3. Increase user sign-ups by 50% within the next 6 months.
  4. Host at least 2 in-person outdoor events, adhering to local health and safety guidelines, to promote the Linkups platform and engage with users face-to-face.
  5. By achieving these goals and objectives, Linkups will be able to establish a strong foundation in New York City, build brand recognition and loyalty among users, and set the stage for continued growth and expansion into new markets in the future.

Design and Development Process

We began our project with brainstorming. It took us a week before we come up with a solution to a problem we need to solve in our community.

In our second class meeting with our professor, I was assigned to lead team one. I assigned multiple roles to my teammates. Leon was a chatbot developer, Sebastian was a powerpoint creator, Hemraj was a writer, Kevin was a web designer and I was a project manager. Roles were not limited to what was aasigned as our professor gave us activities to complete for our project. We eventually got our project name and created a project proposal for Linkups. We continued with vision and mission statements followed by Gantt chart.

Features and Functionality

Linkups will be access on our website and mobile applications. We have hosted our website on Github testing and feedback. Users can book any event on the homepage of our website. Upon RSVPying an event, a section of the booking page will suggest some precautions and tools to use depending on what type of event they are going to. They will be able to search for any events with filtering functionality such as earliest date, nearest location and event type.
