Beberavi

Connecting People. Empowering Communities

Introduction

Beberavi Collections is a fashion brand that offers high quality clothes for children, women and home decorations handwoven, sewed, knitted and crochetted from Kenya, Nakuru.

Our website website let the users aware of what we do and what our missions are. As an intern, I focused on developing a website for Beberavi that speaks its brand and deliver its story to the world.

Project Goals

    Our goal is to tell Beberavi's story to the visitors by:

  1. using colors to represent Beberavi,
  2. showing sustainability, responsibility and Quality,
  3. designing each page with consistency,
  4. and providing information about our founder.
