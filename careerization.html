Careerization

Everyone deserves a profession!

Introduction

Careerization is an online interface designed to assist users in discovering their ideal career and provide them guidance on the necessary steps to become a professional in their chosen field.

Our project will help users find their career and become professionals. Many people are not familiar with what the path they should take to gain skills and knowledge on their careers, so we are here to ensure their options and paths. Our service is available on the website and mobile applications.

Key Tasks of the Web Application

  1. Includes famous peopleâ€™s paths and steps they took so you can have ideas.
  2. Collect user information such as education, work experience, and personal preferences to determine potential career paths.
  3. Use machine learning algorithms and human expertise to match users with career options that align with their interests, skills, and goals.
  4. Provide detailed information on each career option, including salary expectations, job responsibilities, and educational requirements.
  5. Offer guidance on the necessary steps to achieve professional success in a chosen career, such as online courses, certification programs, and industry events.
  6. Allow users to save and compare their preferred career options for future reference.
  7. Connect users with professionals in their chosen career for mentorship or networking opportunities.
  8. Provide personalized recommendations for job openings based on the user's skills and qualifications.
  9. Monitor the user's progress in achieving their career goals and provide feedback or suggestions for improvement.
  10. Continuously update the career options and resources available on the platform based on current job market trends and industry developments.
